The floating meter appears on claude.ai. It reads your usage automatically and keeps it fresh in the background.

Off by default. When on, adds your pay-as-you-go spend (e.g. $0.00 / $30.00) to the meter's detail panel.

Off by default. Claude reports whole-number percentages; when on, the session meter adds an estimated tenths place (e.g. 48.3%) by tracking token use between each 1% step.

Advanced: paste the exact request URL the Usage page loads (from your browser's Network tab) to pin it. Leave blank to auto-detect.
